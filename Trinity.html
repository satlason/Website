Trinity University

Trinity University is located in San Antonio, Texas. This year, 2019 is a big birthday year for Trinity because this year there are 150 years since the school started or in 1869. Trinity is a liberal art institution. The school offers a lot of different type of academic programs. So everybody should be able to find their field for academics here at Trinity.
Trinity also offers five master programs: 1) Master of Arts in School Psychology 2) Master of Arts in Teaching 3) Master of Education in School Leadership 4) Master of Science in Accounting 5) Master of Science in Health Care Administration

San Anotion is the city where Trinity is located. There are a lot of things to do in San Antonio. among one of the most popular thing to do in San Antonio is to go to the River Walk. San Antonio is also the 7th largest city in the U.S. by population with a estimated population of 1,532,233.
